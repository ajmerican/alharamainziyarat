Umrah Registration Terms and Conditions

Payment Terms:

  • Deposit Requirement: A deposit of $1000 per person is required to secure your Umrah registration.
  • Payment Method: All deposits must be paid via e-transfer to admin@travelsolution.ca.
  • Non-Refundable Portion: $500 of the initial $1000 deposit is strictly non-refundable under any circumstances.
  • Balance Payment Deadline: The remaining balance of the total package cost must be paid in full by August 7, 2025.

Cancellation Policy:

  • Cancellation within 15 days before departure: No refund will be issued for cancellations made within 15 days of the scheduled departure date, regardless of the reason for cancellation.
  • Cancellation between 15-30 days before departure: A partial refund may be issued for cancellations made between 15 and 30 days before the scheduled departure date. The exact amount of the partial refund will be determined based on the specific package, incurred costs, and third-party penalties, and will be communicated to the registrant upon cancellation request.
  • Cancellation more than 30 days before departure: For cancellations made more than 30 days before the scheduled departure date, the non-refundable portion of the deposit ($500) will be retained, and the remaining amount of the deposit ($500) will be refunded. Any other payments made beyond the deposit will be fully refunded.

General Terms:

  • All prices are subject to change based on currency fluctuations and supplier cost changes.
  • Travel insurance is highly recommended and may be required for certain packages.
  • Valid passport with at least 6 months validity is required.
  • Saudi visa fees are additional and subject to change.
  • Al Haramain Ziyarat reserves the right to modify itineraries due to circumstances beyond our control.
  • By registering, you agree to abide by all rules and regulations set by Saudi authorities and our religious guides.
